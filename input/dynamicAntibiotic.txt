What is your condition?
--if acute rhinosinusitis, are you allergic to penicillin?
----if yes, doxycycline or a respiratory fluoroquinolone (levofloxacin or moxifloxacin)
----if no, Amoxicillin or amoxicillin/clavulanate
--if Acute uncomplicated bronchitis, then don't take antibiotics
--if cold or respiratory infection, then don't take antibiotics
--if Pharyngitis, then don't take antibiotics
--if Group A beta-hemolytic streptococcal (GAS) infection, are you allergic to penicillin?
----if yes, cephalexin, cefadroxil, clindamycin, or macrolides 
----if no, Amoxicillin and penicillin V
--if Acute uncomplicated cystitis, are you a healthy non-pregnant premenopausal woman?
----if yes, then Nitrofurantoin, trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole (TMP-SMX, where local resistance is <20%), and fosfomycin are appropriate first-line agents.
----if no, then either no antibiotics or Fluoroquinolones (e.g. ciprofloxacin)
