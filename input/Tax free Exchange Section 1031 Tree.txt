------If Yes, Are both properties you are buying and selling to be used productively in a trade or business or for investment purposes?
--------If Yes, Are you buying the property primarily for immediate resale?
----------If Yes, You are not qualified for a tax-free exchange under Section 1031 of the Internal Revenue Code.
----------If No, Are you additionally receiving anything other than the real estate you are purchasing as part of the exchange?
------------If Yes, Did you gain or lose money on the overall exchange (based on the price of the real-estate and fair-market value for any other additional property)?
--------------If Gain, You will need to pay taxes on any gained property other than the real estate you are exchanging.
